The best of ghibli studio 2023

If you want to feel close to the wonderful creations of Studio Ghibli, you can't miss this event. We will celebrate their 38th anniversary by screening 6 of their best creations this October 14 and 15, Mexico City.

2023.10.14(SAT) ~ 15(SUN)

@National Museum of Art, Mexico City, UNAM and more

Main Program

Child drawing

Let your children enjoy drawing and coloring the most famous characters created by Studio Ghibli.

Exhibition photography

You can see images, photographs, posters, sketches, and more in the exhibition mounted inside the museum.

History & context

Expert guests will talk about the history behind each of the films to be screened, behind-the-scenes footage and more.

Movie time

The 6 most popular movies will be screened, such as:
"Mi vecino Totoro
"El viaje de Chihiro" and
"El catillo en el cielo"

Analisis

At the end of each screening, you will be able to discuss the film with one of the Studio Ghibli collaborators, he answer any questions you may have about the film.

Featured speakers

Isao Takahata

Co-founder of Ghibli Studios

His most recognized works were the television series Heidi, the girl from the Alps (1974) and Marco (1976), and the feature films The Grave of the Fireflies (1988), Memories of Yesterday (1991).

Hayao Miyazaki

Co-founder of Ghibli Studios

Internationally renowned Japanese animation film director, animator, illustrator, entrepreneur, manga and anime producer with a career spanning five decades.

Cristina Hernández

Actress and dubbing director

She is recognized for having given her voice to main characters of several anime series such as Sakura Kinomoto, protagonist of Sakura Card Captors, Lima in Saber Marionette, she has also participated in other series such as Pokémon and Digimon..

Toshio Suzuk

Anime film producer

He participated in the premiere of Kunihiko Yuyama's GoShogun The Time Étranger, and in 1986 Suzuki served on the production committee of the Studio Ghibli film The Castle in the Sky.

Mitsuki Nakamura

Animation art director, mechanical designer and illustrator from Tokyo.

Es el director artístico de obras como Mobile Suit Gundam y Nausicaä del valle del viento. Entre sus trabajos como diseñador mecánico caben señalar Kagaku Ninjatai Gatchaman y Mach GoGoGo.

Joe Hisaishi

Japanese composer and orchestra conductor.

Japanese composer and conductor.creator of an extensive body of work since his debut in 1981, he stands out for having composed more than 100 soundtracks for Japanese film directors such as Takeshi Kitano or Yōjirō Takita and, especially, for several Studio Ghibli films directed by Hayao Miyazaki..
